Privacy Policy

Effective 15 June 2026 · Provider: RainnWorks ("we," "us")

TileCam is built to keep your data on your devices. We don't run servers for your video, we don't collect analytics, we don't track you, and there are no accounts. Your camera streams go directly between your Apple device and the camera server you configure — never through us.

The short version: we collect nothing, and we can't see your cameras.

What we collect: nothing

TileCam does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us or to any third party. There is no sign-up, no account, and no analytics or advertising SDKs in the app. We cannot see your cameras, your streams, or your server.

Your video and audio

TileCam connects to the go2rtc / WebRTC camera server that you set up and point it at. All live video and audio flows directly between your device and that server, on your local network or wherever you host it. It is never routed to, recorded by, or stored by us.

Information stored only on your device

Your settings — your server address and app preferences — are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. If you use the Apple Watch companion, those settings and the live frames are sent between your iPhone and your own paired Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity; they do not pass through us.

Microphone

If you use live audio with a camera that supports it, TileCam uses the microphone only for that real-time WebRTC audio session. Audio is not recorded or saved by the app, and is not sent to us.

Local network

TileCam asks for local-network access so it can reach camera servers running on your home or office network. This is used solely to connect to the servers you configure.

Tracking

We do not track you across apps or websites, and the app contains no tracking technologies. Our App Store privacy disclosures and the privacy manifest bundled with the app reflect this.

Payments

The Apple Watch unlock is sold by Apple as the merchant of record and processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive your name, payment-card details, billing address, or any other personal data from a purchase — only anonymised, aggregated sales figures from Apple. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

EEA & UK users (GDPR)

RainnWorks is the data controller for TileCam. Because the app does not collect or transmit personal data to us, we hold no personal data about you to access, correct, export, or delete. We do not profile you, make automated decisions about you, or transfer personal data internationally — there is no personal data to process, so no lawful basis under the GDPR or UK GDPR is engaged.

For completeness, if you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, the right to data portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Any in-app purchase is handled by Apple as an independent controller (see Payments above).

California users (CCPA / CPRA)

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information, as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the CPRA. We have not sold or shared personal information in the preceding twelve months and do not do so. California residents have the right to know, delete, and correct personal information, and to opt out of its sale or sharing — but because we collect none, there is nothing to disclose, delete, or opt out of. We will never discriminate against you for exercising a privacy right.

Children

TileCam does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Privacy questions, or a request relating to the rights above, can be sent to support@rainn.works. As we hold no personal data, most such requests simply confirm there is nothing for us to action.
